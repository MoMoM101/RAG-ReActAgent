# Cardiovascular Drug Prescribing Reference

## Atorvastatin Calcium
Brand: Lipitor
Class: HMG-CoA reductase inhibitor (statin)
Strengths: 10mg/tab, 20mg/tab, 40mg/tab
Dosing: oral, start 10mg QD, max 80mg QD
Contraindications: active liver disease, Pregnancy Category X, lactation
ADR: myalgia (incidence 3-5%), ALT elevation (>3xULN requires discontinuation),
  rhabdomyolysis rare (<0.1%)
Monitoring: LFT (baseline+4wks), CK (if muscle symptoms), LDL-C (4-12wks follow-up)
Drug Interactions: CYP3A4 substrate - clarithromycin/itraconazole co-administration
  increases myopathy risk
Storage: 20-25C, protect from light, sealed container

## Clopidogrel
Brand: Plavix
Class: P2Y12 receptor antagonist (antiplatelet)
Strengths: 75mg/tab, 300mg/tab (loading dose)
Dosing: loading dose 300mg PO once, maintenance 75mg QD
Contraindications: active bleeding (peptic ulcer, intracranial hemorrhage),
  severe hepatic impairment (Child-Pugh Class C)
ADR: bleeding (incidence 2-3%), TTP (extremely rare), agranulocytosis (<0.1%)
Monitoring: platelet count (if bleeding symptoms), CYP2C19 genotype (poor responders)
Drug Interactions: omeprazole reduces antiplatelet activity (avoid co-administration),
  warfarin increases bleeding risk
Storage: 15-30C, protect from moisture

## Nifedipine GITS
Brand: Adalat
Class: dihydropyridine calcium channel blocker (CCB)
Strengths: 30mg/tab, 60mg/tab
Dosing: start 30mg QD, max 90mg QD, swallow whole do not crush or split
Contraindications: cardiogenic shock, unstable angina, severe aortic stenosis,
  first trimester pregnancy
ADR: headache/facial flushing (10-15%), ankle edema (5-8%), hypotension (2%),
  gingival hyperplasia (long-term use)
Monitoring: blood pressure (each visit), heart rate, lower extremity edema
Drug Interactions: strong CYP3A4 inhibitors (ketoconazole) increase plasma concentration,
  grapefruit juice is contraindicated
Storage: below 25C, protect from light
